**Project Brief: WhatsApp AI Business Assistant (Evara AI Extension)**

## Overview

We are building a WhatsApp-based AI assistant that can automatically respond to customer messages on behalf of a business. The goal is to create a simple, fast, and scalable system that helps businesses handle customer queries without manual intervention.

---

## Core Idea

The system will connect with WhatsApp and automatically reply to incoming customer messages using AI.
The AI should generate responses based on the business information provided by the user.

A key focus is simplicity, usability, and multilingual support for Indian users.

---

## Key Features (MVP)

1. **WhatsApp Integration**

   * Connect business WhatsApp using WhatsApp Cloud API
   * Ability to receive and send messages automatically

2. **AI Auto Reply System**

   * Automatically respond to incoming messages
   * Responses should be short, natural, and human-like

3. **Business Information Setup**

   * Users can input structured business details such as:

     * Business name
     * Type
     * Working hours
     * Location
     * Services/products

4. **Business Knowledge Book (Important Feature)**

   * A large input area where users can describe their business in detail
   * AI should use this as the primary knowledge source for generating responses
   * This allows flexible, dynamic, and intelligent replies

5. **Multi-language Support (Critical)**

   * The AI must detect and respond in the user’s language automatically
   * Support for Hindi, English, Hinglish, and regional Indian languages (Marathi, Telugu, Tamil, etc.)

6. **Basic Chat Logs**

   * Display conversation history between customers and AI
   * Useful for monitoring responses

7. **Basic Controls**

   * Toggle auto-reply ON/OFF
   * Select tone (friendly/professional)
   * Language preference (auto/manual)

---

## Admin / Business Owner Capabilities

* Connect/disconnect WhatsApp
* Configure business details
* Write and update Business Knowledge Book
* View chat logs
* Control AI behavior (on/off, tone, language)

---

## Technical Direction (High-Level)

* Use WhatsApp Cloud API for messaging
* Backend should handle webhook events from WhatsApp
* AI response generation will be handled by the existing Evara AI system
* Store business data and chat logs in database
* Ensure scalability and clean architecture

---

## Design & Implementation Flexibility

* The developer/team has full freedom in terms of UI/UX, layout, and structure
* Wireframes are not fixed — feel free to design the best possible experience
* Focus should be on usability, clarity, and performance

---

## Goal

To build a simple but powerful AI assistant that can:

* Save time for businesses
* Improve response speed
* Handle multilingual conversations
* Act as a virtual customer support agent on WhatsApp

---

## Notes

* Start with a clean MVP (avoid overbuilding)
* Advanced features can be added in later phases
* Priority: reliability, speed, and simplicity

---
